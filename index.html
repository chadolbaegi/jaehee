mc stands for "main character" and is the protagonist of the game Mystic Messenger. she stumbles into the RFA chatroom by accident and ends up taking up former member/founder Rika's place to organize RFA's big party. she is meant as a stand-in character and has no official name or appearance, but the one that is most commonly pictured in the game is one with long straight brown hair.
